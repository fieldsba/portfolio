All About Alex


Hello! My name is Alex, and I am an aspiring software engineer! After spending six years working in healthcare, I realized it was time for a change. One of my favorite aspects of my job as a radiologic technologist was solving problems to acquire diagnostic-quality images. When I realized it was time for a change, I knew I wanted a career in which I could continue my passion for creative problem solving. I was introduced to the tech world by family members and immediately fell in love. I completed Vanderbilt University's Full-Stack Web Development Bootcamp in 2022 and I am so excited to be beginning my journey as a software engineer! I've always had a passion for video games, and I would love to one day learn to use my programming skills to develop video games.

Taskinator

Built with JavaScript, HTML, and CSS, this app allows the user to keep a to-do list in the style of a kanban board by saving and loading tasks from localStorage. It features tasks sorted into three columns: tasks to do, tasks in progress, and completed tasks. Personally, I find that looking at a list of my completed tasks fills me with a sense of pride and accomplishment at how far I've come in my tasks for the day!

React Flashcards

React Flashcards is an app built using ReactJS and the Open Trivia Database API. The app allows the user to generate a chosen number of flashcards with questions and answer choices from 24 different categories. Like with any flashcards, the questions and answer choices are shown on the front of the card, and the correct answer is shown on the back of the card, which is revealed by clicking. I am a huge trivia buff and Jeopardy fan and had a ton of fun creating this project!
